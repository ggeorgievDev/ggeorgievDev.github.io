Effective Date: 13th September 2023

George Georgiev ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our mobile application, Gallery Slide (the "App"). We do not collect or connect any user data when you use our App.

We want to make it clear that we do not collect or connect any personally identifiable information (PII) or user data when you use our App, including but not limited to:

Information Security:

We take the security of your non-existent data seriously. Even though we do not collect any user data, we implement reasonable security measures to protect the App and the data it does not collect from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Our App may contain links to third-party websites or services. Please note that we do not have control over the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they collect and use your information, even though we don't.

Children's Privacy:

Thank you for using Gallery Slide! We respect your privacy, even though we don't collect any of your data.
